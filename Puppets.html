2022 Faculty & Staff Exhibition Takeaways

These are ten paper puppets that I made for Graphic Design class in February 2022. The perimeters of the assignment were to make takeaways out of paper for the schoolâ€™s Annual Art + Design Faculty & Staff Exhibition. I got the idea for three finger puppets of the main art professors from a daydream where I made a puppet who looked like my professor mock him. I thought if I made a puppet that looked like him and made him talk that would be funny. I borrowed their structure from a YouTube video on how to make finger puppets out of recycled materials. After the class discussion about the projects, I decided it would be an even better idea to make puppets out of the work. I extended the partial body characters into full bodies. I tried to make the style of the face muppet style. I colored them with color pencils. I wanted to make something entertaining that people could enjoy and would remind them of the artwork.
