The Smartphone Trap #1 & #2

For the Editorial Illustration unit in Illustration, the requirements were to choose an editorial article and create an illustration to go along with it. I chose "The Smartphone Trap," a guest essay published in The New York Times on Sunday, August 1, 2021, by Jonathan Haidt and Jean M. Twenge. It is about the negative effects on teenagers of using a phone. In #1, the smartphone traps the user in a bubble, isolating her from human connection. In #2, the smartphone controls the user like a puppet because the article says too much screen time on social media makes teenagers feel bad about themselves when they see their peers seemingly picture-perfect lives. Thus, the smartphone controls the world they see.
