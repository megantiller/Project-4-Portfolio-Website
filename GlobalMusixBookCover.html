Global Musix Book Cover

For Intro to Graphic Communications class, I had to design book covers. A faculty member approached my professor, seeking a student to make a book cover for her Opensource textbook on global music. My professor decided to hold a contest between the members of the class to determine who gets to design it. This book cover was one of my strongest ideas.

I tried to be creative by designing something other than a globe and music notes, so I centered my idea around headphones. There are two halves of the Earth on each side of the headphones to relate the drawing to the world. I made the energy from the music radiate out from the headphones and rise to the top of the page because I needed to make the composition dynamic. I do not remember how I made it look loose.
