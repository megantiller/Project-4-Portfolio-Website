Greeting Cards

I made these cards for the Hallmark Graphic Design Internship Application. I examined Hallmark's collection of Chinese New Year Cards and then tried to take a livelier approach. When I was in first grade, I read a book to the class where the pig sat in a bath of vegetables and did not know a wolf was trying to cook him. I applied this to a turkey. I was trying to think of creative concepts. After thinking of several ideas, the idea of a teacher as a superhero came to me. I made the bunny one as part of a volunteer project hosted at my school on Valentine’s Day. We made cards and care packages for sexual abuse survivors. I began the bunny one by finding a line in the suggestions for what to write to someone on Valentine's Day on Hallmark’s website. I thought something funny would fulfill the goal of giving them a lift, so the line about the bunny sounded right. I expanded upon the concept by making the bunny show his love by going to where children are drawing with chalk to make a heart out of carrots.
